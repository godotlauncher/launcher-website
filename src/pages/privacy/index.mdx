# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 24, 2025_

Godot Launcher ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy
Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our
website at [https://godotlauncher.org](https://godotlauncher.org).

By using this website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Godot Launcher is an open-source project maintained by an Mario Debono based in
Malta. The project is publicly accessible and licensed under the MIT License.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information (PII) directly
through our website. We do **not** have user accounts, forms, or subscriptions.

However, we use third-party analytics tools that may collect limited data for
statistical and functional purposes. This data includes:

- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Clicks and scrolls
- Device and browser information
- General location based on IP address

We do not link this data to any user profile or identify you personally.

---

## 3. Use of Cookies

We use cookies for the sole purpose of analytics and improving your experience
on the site. You will be asked to provide your consent before any non-essential
cookies are set.

Cookies may include:

- Essential cookies required for core site functionality
- Analytics cookies used to measure site performance and usage patterns

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the consent banner
provided, `Cookie Settings` link in the site footer, or by adjusting your
browser settings.

---

## 4. Analytics and Third-Party Services

We use third-party analytics providers to better understand how visitors use
our site. These services may store information such as your IP address, browser
type, and interaction data.

The data is processed in an anonymized and aggregated form and is used solely
for the purpose of improving our website.

These third-party services are subject to their own privacy policies and data
protection practices.

We do not share or sell any information to third parties.

---

## 5. Hosting

by GitHub Inc. GitHub may collect basic logs and technical data as part of its
standard hosting operation. Please refer to
[GitHub's PrivacyStatement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement)
for more details.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR & UK GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom,
you have the following rights under applicable data protection laws:

- **Right of Access** – You can request access to any personal data that may be
  held about you.
- **Right to Rectification** – You can ask us to correct or update inaccurate
  or incomplete data.
- **Right to Erasure** – You may request deletion of any personal data, where
  applicable.
- **Right to Restriction** – You can request limitations on how your data is
  processed.
- **Right to Object** – You may object to certain processing of your data,
  including for analytics.
- **Right to Data Portability** – You may request a copy of your data in a
  portable format.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent** – You can withdraw consent at any time where
  processing is based on consent.

We do not store any personal data directly, and the third-party
analytics tools we use do not collect personally identifiable
information. As such, there is no personal data held by us or these tools that
can be accessed or deleted.

for questions, contact us at [privacy@godotlauncher.org](mailto:privacy@godotlauncher.org).

If you are a resident of California, you may have additional rights under the

- The right to request deletion of your data
- The right to opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell
  personal data)
- The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

We do not store any personal data directly, and the third-party
analytics tools we use do not collect personally identifiable
information. As such, there is no personal data held by us or these tools that
can be accessed or deleted.

You can contact us at
[privacy@godotlauncher.org](mailto:privacy@godotlauncher.org) for any related
