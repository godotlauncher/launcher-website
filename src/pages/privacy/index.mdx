# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 3, 2026_

Godot Launcher ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy
Policy explains what information may be processed when you use our website at
[https://godotlauncher.org](https://godotlauncher.org).

By using this website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Godot Launcher is an open source project maintained by Mario Debono and
contributors. The project is publicly accessible and licensed under the MIT
License.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

We do **not** collect personally identifiable information directly through
website accounts, forms, or subscriptions. This website does not provide user
accounts, account profiles, contact forms, or mailing list signup forms.

However, analytics, cookies, and hosting services may process limited technical
or usage data, including:

- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Clicks and scrolls
- Device and browser information
- Approximate location inferred from IP address
- Basic hosting logs and request metadata

We do not use this information to create user accounts or personal profiles.

---

## 3. Use of Cookies

We use cookies for site functionality, analytics, and understanding how the
website is used. You will be asked to provide consent before non-essential
cookies are set.

Cookies may include:

- Essential cookies required for core site functionality
- Analytics cookies used to measure site performance and usage patterns

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the consent banner,
the `Cookie Settings` link in the site footer, or your browser settings.

---

## 4. Analytics and Third-Party Services

We use third-party analytics providers to better understand how visitors use
the website. These services may process information such as IP address, browser
type, device information, visited pages, and interaction data.

Analytics data is used to understand website performance and improve content.
These third-party services are subject to their own privacy policies and data
protection practices.

We do not sell personal information.

---

## 5. Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a static site hosting service provided
by GitHub Inc. GitHub may process basic logs and technical data as part of its
standard hosting operation. Please refer to
[GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement)
for more details.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR and UK GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom,
you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, including:

- **Right of Access** - You can request access to personal data that may be
  held about you.
- **Right to Rectification** - You can ask for inaccurate or incomplete data to
  be corrected.
- **Right to Erasure** - You can request deletion of personal data, where
  applicable.
- **Right to Restriction** - You can request limits on how personal data is
  processed.
- **Right to Object** - You may object to certain processing, including
  analytics processing.
- **Right to Data Portability** - You may request a copy of personal data in a
  portable format, where applicable.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent** - You can withdraw consent at any time where
  processing is based on consent.

Because this website does not provide accounts, forms, or subscriptions, we may
not hold account records or directly submitted personal data about you. For
analytics, cookies, or hosting data, available options may depend on the
consent tool, browser controls, and the relevant third-party service provider.

For privacy questions, contact us at
[privacy@godotlauncher.org](mailto:privacy@godotlauncher.org).

## 7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA as amended)

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the California
Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended, where the law applies. These rights may
include:

- The right to know what personal information is collected and how it is used
- The right to request deletion of personal information, subject to exceptions
- The right to correct inaccurate personal information
- The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information
- The right to limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information,
  where applicable
- The right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

We do not sell personal information. We do not provide website accounts, forms,
or subscriptions that knowingly collect sensitive personal information from
visitors.

For related inquiries, contact us at
[privacy@godotlauncher.org](mailto:privacy@godotlauncher.org).
